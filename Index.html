Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get an eye exam?
Adults should have an eye exam at least once every 1–2 years. Children and people with vision problems may need more frequent checkups.
Do you provide emergency eye care?
Yes, if you experience sudden vision loss, eye injury, or severe discomfort, contact us immediately. We provide same-day emergency eye care.
Can kids get their eyes checked here?
Absolutely! We offer pediatric eye exams and vision screenings to help children achieve healthy eyesight and succeed in school.
Do you prescribe glasses and contact lenses?
Yes, our optometrists provide accurate prescriptions and we offer a wide selection of glasses and contact lenses to suit your needs.
Can eye exams detect other health problems?
Yes, eye exams can help detect conditions like diabetes, high blood pressure, and glaucoma early, making them a vital part of overall health care.
